The following tools support your strategy and help you achieve it:

  • Projects
  • Objectives
  • Metrics and Targets
  • Activities

Projects
A project is a group of activities/tasks that moves the organization toward its strategic goal.

For example, the strategic goal to "become the region's dominant integrated engineering design company in 3 years" would mean working on these three projects:

  1. Expand the current facility and add new equipment
  2. Improve production efficiency
  3. Increase marketing efforts
  4. Note: when discussing strategy, projects are sometimes referred to as 'themes'.
